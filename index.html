What is SI Coaching?

Strategic Coaching is a dynamic type of life coaching which is used worldwide to help individuals, families, couples, and organizations to support the individual and group. It brings a system of change that empowers you to take action in your daily life. I firmly believe that all people have the potential to overcome the negative patterns and beliefs which keep them from thriving and reaching their goals.

group

How can I help?

I've gone through intensive study and training in strategies to handle the whole range of human needs and life challenges. Instead of focusing on one type of result, I address the wide range of human potential and obstacles. I identify obstacles (such as your internal beliefs, communication patterns, relationships, and life stages) and prescribe practical strategies to create results in each of these areas.

settings

What can you expect?

You can expect to gain clarity, action, and progress towards the goals you want to achieve. I teach you to learn about yourself and how to effectively impact the key people in your life. Do you want to rebuild a difficult relationship, become more inspired about your career, or follow through on a plan? If you answered yes, then join our community of clients that have discovered areas of opportunity, and more insight and harmony with those they care about.
